Meet Martin

Distinguished Leadership

Dr. Martin Lemelle, Jr. serves as the 11th President of Grambling State University, bringing a wealth of experience in higher education administration and financial excellence to one of America's premier historically black colleges and universities.

Financial Growth

Educational Excellence

Throughout his distinguished career, Dr. Lemelle has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to student success, institutional growth, and academic excellence. His strategic vision has transformed multiple institutions through innovative programs and sustainable financial management.

"Education is the foundation upon which we build our future. Every student deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential."

Vision for the Future

Under Dr. Lemelle's leadership, Grambling State University continues to expand its reach and impact, preparing students for success in an ever-evolving global economy while maintaining its proud tradition of excellence and service.
